.OVERVIEW
--------
     
A world-class interactive, real-time voice translation technology supporting 20 languages, including Chinese, that can also generate CC files in 70 languages as people speak in real-time.
.  
.Some of the features:
.   
  -  Converse with colleagues wherever they are in any language
  -  Present online webinars and have collaboration sessions in different languages, such as Chinese
  -  Enhance your CRM capabilities by talking to your customers in their language
  -  Provide captions and subtitled video in an automated manner
.         
.VOICE TRANSLATION
-----------------
This software enables multi-language communication at high speeds. Both your voice and the voice of the other person is translated at the same time, including both audio and text. All users have control over the quality of the translation. This software is powerful enough to understand you and will improve every time you use it.
.                
.ANYONE CAN USE RTT
------------------
We offer a simple version that can be used immediately by virtually anyone to converse with others who speak a different language. The system can also be personalized to your voice and the way you speak with a little extra time and effort. The system uses synthesized voices to output the translated text or spoken words. No download or installation is required!
.                 
.THE TECHNOLOGY
--------------
The technology connects every major player in the language automation field: Android, Apple, Google, Microsoft, Nuance, etc. Each time you use the system it gives you multiple ways to improve the quality of the results. By involving all of the technology leaders in the space, our design assures that it functions on almost every modern device from desktop to smartphone.
